Features

Everything a desktop OCR should do

From a quick screen grab to batches of PDFs — recognize, verify and export in one window.

📄

Complex document recognition

Multi-column layouts, tables, formulas and handwriting go to the PaddleOCR-VL vision-language model; the source page and recognition regions sit side by side for page-by-page verification.

📊

Tables to CSV · Formulas to LaTeX

Export tables straight to CSV for Excel; copy formulas as LaTeX for your paper. Whole pages export as Markdown, JSON or TXT.

✂️

Capture & recognize

Ctrl+Alt+S selects any screen region — recognized text lands in your clipboard with a notification.

🗂️

Batch queue

Drop multiple images/PDFs to queue them automatically, with retry, cancel and resume across restarts.

🔍

Full-text history

Everything you've recognized is searchable (SQLite FTS5) — or leave no trace with privacy mode.

Interface

Simple, not simplistic

Light/dark themes, Chinese & English UI, tray residency — desktop details done right.

Privacy notice

Images and documents are uploaded to Baidu Cloud for recognition; your access token lives only in Windows Credential Manager and is never written to files or logs. If a file must stay on your device, use an offline OCR tool.
